Why am I Here?

I am here to change my stars. I have been wanting to change my career for a long time. Or rather, actually choose a career rather than just stay where I've landed. As I was trying to decide whether to take this course or not I watched "A Knights Tale". If you haven't seen it, watch it! It makes me laugh! As I watched I deciced I was going to do it. I decided to change my stars. I am here to learn a marketable, in demand skill, and find a new career.
